About

Drop a screenshot or PDF of any event — a flight confirmation, dinner reservation, hotel booking, concert tickets — and this app will read the details and add it to your Google Calendar.

You can also paste confirmation text directly from an email, or take a photo on mobile.

If your input contains multiple events, each one will be detected and shown as a separate card you can review and add individually or all at once.

Your files are processed entirely in your browser using OCR. Nothing is uploaded or stored anywhere.
